FREELANCE PROJECTS

At Taqwa Mosque

At Taqwa Mosque façade design was requested by Mr. Bowo, the owner of Redjo Mulyo and Sambas family member, whose famous in Purbalingga. This mosque project was designed in 2017 and located in Kalimanah, Purbalingga, Jawa Tengah. Simplicity was the main concept of this mosque. Modern design with a touch of middle-eastern architecture was brought to this building. Big simple geometry like rectangle and horizontal-vertical line added modern element to the facade. Islamic geometry pattern that shown at the front of the building and the middle-eastern curve at the opening was designed to bring middle-eastern feel to the mosque. But this mosque also still maintaining local architectural culture with a traditional roof at the top.

Baiturrahman Mosque

Baiturrahman Mosque façade design was requested by Mr. Arief. This mosque project was designed in 2017 and located in Babakan, Purbalingga, Jawa Tengah. With a slightly low budget, Mr. Arief requested this mosque to be simple but with a modern and natural look. This mosque its traditional roof as a cultural tolerance because this isn’t Jami’ Mosque, which is usually dedicated as a symbolic mosque at certain region; but this mosque is mere local small mosque as we call it Mushola. Modern look is projected by certain simple geometric and pattern such as rectangle and horizontal-vertical line. And natural look of this mosque is projected by its material choice such as natural rock as a façade.

Didin House

Mr. Didin’s House was requested of course by Mr. Didin himself. This house project was designed in 2016 and located in Sukabumi, Jawa Barat. With only 126 m2 site, Mr. Didin requested me to design a one level floor conventional house that can afford a family of a couple with 2 childs. This house design concept is a medditeranian theme with a slightly modern touch. The room of this house consist of 3 bedrooms, one living room, one guest room, 2 bathrooms, one kitchen and dining room, one mushola, and one garden in the center-back of the building as opening and air flow.

Bayu House Renovation

Mr. Bayu requested to renovate his house by adding a second floor and redesign its façade. This renovation project was designed in March, 2019 and located in Pondok Pinang, Jakarta Selatan. This project has a site of 222m2 and +200m2 of total floor area. The layout concept of this building is efficiency, meaning that with such a small site this house can afford many rooms that can accommodate a family with a son plus a rent-able area. The façade of this building has a horizontal-vertical line pattern concept that can projecting modern aura.

Fickry House

This conventional house design was requested by Mr. Fickry. This project was designed in December, 2019 and located in Babakan, Purbalingga, Jawa Tengah. Its site is huge for a conventional house with an area of 339 m2. And because of that, Mr. Fickry requested to design a house that can accommodate a family of a couple with 3 childs but only on one level floor. This house contains 5 bedrooms, one living room, one guest room, 3 bathrooms, one kitchen and dining room, one garage, one mushola, and one private garden at the back of the house. Concept of this house is modern minimalistic with a color scheme of black and white. Simple geometry with a slight touch of a line passing through is adding its modern element.

Kitchen Set Interior

This kitchen set interior design was requested by Mrs. Desty. This project was designed in March, 2020 and located in Pekayon Jaya, Bekasi, Jawa Barat. This small interior project with an area of 15 m2 hopefully can accommodate a full set of kitchen equipment. The concept of this kitchen set interior is modern minimalistic with a color scheme mostly white and a little touch of natural material such as stone wall and counter, and wood layer in the cabinet and shelves.

COMPETITION PROJECTS

Darussalam Mosque Redesign

This is an architectural competition project called Sayembara Re-Desain Rumah Ibadah yang Aksesibel untuk Semua, held by DRIA MANUNGGAL and PUSTRAL UGM. This competition was about redesigning a praying place (mosque) that can be accessible by all kind of people, especially people with disability. This competition was held in 2017 and my chosen site was Darussalam Mosque located in Ngaglik, Sleman, Daerah Istimewa Yogyakarta. The reason why I chose this mosque was that this mosque has a big different floor level than the road at the front, it has 1.5 m lower depth differential. Thus, it must be challenging to be accessed by everyone that has disability. So, I redesign its building by adding some barrier-free feature such as ramp, railing, different floor texture for blind people, etc. Its façade also redesigned with simple minimalistic white concept with a slightly Islamic Geometrical Pattern and curve. And this mosque project won the Media Advokasi Category Winner in this architectural competition.
